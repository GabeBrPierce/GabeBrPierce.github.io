About

Hi, my name is Tyler Boydstun. I began drumming at the age of 12 at Mount Ogden Jr High School. I went on to play in the Ogden High School Marching Band for 3 years, and the Ogden High School Winter Drumline for 2. This is when I started taking private lessons with local legend, Louise Schouten. I also spent 2 years in the Ben Lomond High School Bagpipe Corps. While at Ben Lomond, I continued taking lessons with Louise, who had me study rudimental snare solos. I earned a spot to compete in the State Solo and Ensemble in both my Junior and Senior year, as well as a chance to participate in the IMPA Solo event both those years. After High School, I dove headfirst into the Pipe Band world. This meant investing in private lessons with Steven McWhirter, the 10x and current World Solo Drumming Champion. I also took this time to establish an Alumni Pipe Band and feeder program in the local School District. I currently perform and compete in the Ulster Scottish Pipe Band located in Glenside, PA under leading drummer Derek Cooper. I work closely with Chris Livingstone, owner and founder of the Ogden Drum Studio, to further develop my own skills on the drumset. He has helped me to establish a teaching curriculum for beginning through advanced level drumset students and has given me the push I needed to start teaching. I use my expertise in the fundamentals and rudiments of drumming to push students. Whether they are beginning, intermediate, or advanced players, I help all levels and all styles of students to develop the skills needed to achieve higher levels of playing.
